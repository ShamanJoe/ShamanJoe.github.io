About me

Background

I'm an outgoing guy, with a lots of energy and very sociable (I hope). I don't hesitate to put on a smile every day and I take a lot of pride in the fact that I try to be myself 100%, all the time. 22 years old, grew up in a small place called Stridsklev, in Porsgrunn. Growing up, I grew roots in front of the screen, whether it was PlayStation or computer. The times I was kicked out by my mother because she was tired of me sitting inside, I was out rocking the streets with my buddies watching the grass grow and kicking pebbles along the road. Currently located in sunny Tønsberg with my girlfriend and our dog. My fascination for computers in an early age was the reason I went further in to the technologial world. I always wanted to create my own games when I was younger and that's how I got in to programming. This is when I discovered a whole world filled with opportunities. I am doing a bachelors in frontend and mobile development at Høyskolen Kristiania, in Oslo. Currently on my third and last year. Here I have acquired a lot of good knowledge, as well as a lot of practical experience in the form of work requirements, exam tasks etc. I am happy to challenge myself and love to learn. I would like to learn as much as possible about everything possible, so that I can build myself up and apply my knowledge in a constructive way..
